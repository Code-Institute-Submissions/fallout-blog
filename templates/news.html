Best Weapon

There are literally hundreds of weapons in the Fallout games, So lots to choose from. For me It would have to be either the 2076 world series baseball bat, Just because of how fun it is to see enemies literally fly away or the gainer. Small, simple, effective, I like it.

The conclusion of the whole storyline was so bizzare and funny that it's something I will never forget!
